Project Idea

Overview

Health care home automation A.I system which would be a package made up of multiple devices working together to maintain a constant monitoring system while person/persons are home. This will involve a system being setup in your home to watch and monitor movements and vital signs through an A.I system and cameras with sensors, a smart watch, and a phone application which would be free to all customers who purchase this package. This design would be for people with disabilities such as PTSD, epileptic, elderly and really anyone that suffers from something that could cause significant injury or even death that needs/wants to monitor their conditions in their home and when they are alone.

Motivation

This would be very use project because this would help so many people and relieve a lot of stress from family members that would be worrying about loved ones. From my research there is an article from The World Health Organisation stating Northern Ireland (UK), United Kingdom of Great Britain and the Canada and Australia for people 60 years or older range from 1.6 to 3.0 per 10 000 population are being admitted to hospital for fall. Just to think about what I could do to prevent my grandparents from going through this has given me the idea and motivation to come up with this idea. The use of things like raspberry Pi technology would be used in the creation of this would save money and time and make it more affordable for me the designer and the customers.

Description

This product will feature 24/7 home monitoring when you are home, this will be possible with the smart watch features and phone application that will let the system know when you have entered or exited your home. This system will be made using multiple Raspberry Pi systems setup up around your home. A.I system installed to monitor and track movements and vitals and monitor body temperature through the inferred sensors, cameras and the sensors build into your smart watch. The A.I system will be designed to do a scan of you when it is first setup to help keep track of the right person. This program will be designed to interact with its user, if decided to activate this feature you could ask for weather report, call family/friends via connection to your phone through the application provided. During the setup you will be prompted to fill out an emergency contact section which will include email and contact number as this will be used for one of the features of safety alerts to emergency contact if anything happens example you fall and hurt yourself and you need assistance but cannot get to the phone or the system picks up on you having a epileptic fit in which it will sent out an SOS to your emergency contact and contact emergency services. With this A.I design it will be able to learn movement patterns like the way you walk, so if you hurt your leg, it would be able to re-adjust your movement pattern to assess if you have injured for self and how severe it maybe. The application for your phone will be free to download, this will feature monitoring system where you can share details like vitals and camera footage to your emergency contact so they can keep an eye on your and know everything is ok. You can store data of your vitals to show and share with medical professionals any time you want. This will include 24/7 technical services and constant bug fixes to make sure it runs without any problems. The application is yours and no one can see any information as we will provide an encryption to stop anyone from to the access it. The Raspberry Pi system will feature cameras and sensors to which you choose where you want the cameras setup and the sensors, this will come in different size packs to suit different sized homes so if you want a camera in every room but still want your privacy in certain rooms then you can set that camera up to sensors only, that way it can still pick up if you have a fall for example. The watch you will be able to use any smart watch that can access the application to link up and relay data it collects like rise in heart rate or your sleep cycle, this in turn will help the A.I learn and monitor more accurately to these results and patterns relayed to the system.

Tools and Technologies

The infrared Raspberry Pi Camera Module 2 NoIR- this can be added to the Raspberry Pi as a camera with inferred for uses at night-time. Raspberry Pi is the main component for the system, as it’s the computer that will store the A.I on it and operate the system. The MQ gas sensors can detect different gases at room temperature, this will be a handle addition to the package for the elderly who for get to turn the stove off. The PIR motion sensor - a signal is sent only if something moves. It can be uses to activate the lighting or to turn on touch screen for home automation as soon as you get close to it. 2.4 GHz NRF24L01+ Modul is a wireless communication addon that uses 2.4 GHz frequency, this means it can transfer larger amount of data. A second Raspberry Pi can also be equipped with a 2.4 GHz receiver / transmitter and get commands from a “base station” and send back data. TensorFlow is an open source artificial intelligence software. that helps you develop and train Machine learning models, this free AI software. This software you can develop voice/sound recognition, text-based applications, image recognition, video detection.
